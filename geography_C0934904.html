Dahod, Gujarat, India

Dahod is located on the border of Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh.It has the rich history and a vibrant culture. My city is famous for one of the lakes which is surrounded by the beautiful garden and the bicycle track.

Dahod is also famous for its medical and engineering institutions and it also well connected by rail and road.

Famous place in Dahod

Ratanmahal WildLife Sanctuary is the renowned place in my home city.

This place is known for its WildLife, ancient temples and picturesque landscapes which attracts many pilgrims and tourists.The sanctuary features a beautiful waterfall, creating a serene and picturesque setting.

The place with its lush forested hills and diverse wildlife, it's a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Visitors can enjoy trekking, wildlife spotting, and the peaceful ambiance of this captivating natural retreat.
