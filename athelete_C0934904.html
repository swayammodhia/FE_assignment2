Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov, a retired mixed martial artist from Dagestan, Russia, is celebrated for his perfect record and incredible grappling abilities. As the UFC Lightweight Champion, he defeated notable opponents such as Conor McGregor and Dustin Poirier. Known for his relentless pressure and outstanding wrestling, Khabib retired in 2020 with an unblemished 29-0 record. His legacy extends beyond his victories; he is admired for his sportsmanship, dedication to his faith, and commitment to his family.
